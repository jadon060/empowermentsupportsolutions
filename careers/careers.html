About the Role

Empowerment Support Solutions (ESS) partners with families and Regional Centers to deliver individualized supports under California’s Self-Determination Program (SDP). Direct Support Professionals (DSPs) provide one-on-one assistance in home and community settings, helping participants build real-world skills like budgeting, housekeeping, safety awareness, and transportation.

Qualifications

  • Minimum 1 year experience supporting individuals with developmental disabilities (paid or lived experience).
  • Reliable transportation & smartphone for documentation and communication.
  • Current CPR/First Aid certification required (or willing to obtain within 30 days).
  • Spanish or bilingual skills are a plus.
