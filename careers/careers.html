About the Role

Empowerment Support Solutions (ESS) works with adults with developmental and mental disabilities to help them live more independent, confident lives. As an Independent Living Support Specialist, you will work one on one with a client in their home and out in the community, helping them learn everyday life skills like cooking simple meals, cleaning their space, using public transportation, managing money, and staying safe. Your role is to support, encourage, and guide clients as they practice these skills in real life, building confidence and independence over time. ESS is currently building its initial team for upcoming client activations. Candidates selected during this phase will be contacted as services go live.

Why Work With ESS

  • Structured, consistent schedules built in coordination with the client and their family.
  • Competitive pay with higher rates for experienced and bilingual specialists.
  • One-on-one client support so you build real relationships and see real progress.
  • Performance bonuses and annual pay increases based on reliability and quality of care.
  • Get out in the community helping people build independence and confidence in real-world settings.
  • Opportunity to grow with a new and expanding organization.

Qualifications

  • Minimum 1 year experience supporting individuals with developmental disabilities (paid or lived experience).
  • Reliable transportation & smartphone for documentation and communication.
  • Current CPR/First Aid certification required (or willing to obtain within 30 days).
  • Spanish or bilingual skills are a plus.
