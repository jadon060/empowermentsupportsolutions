About the Role

Empowerment Support Solutions (ESS) partners with families and Regional Centers to deliver individualized skills training under the Self-Determination Program (SDP). Direct Support Professionals (DSPs) provide one-on-one support in home and community settings, helping clients build life skills like budgeting, housekeeping, and transportation.

Qualifications

  • Experience supporting individuals with developmental disabilities.
  • Reliable transportation & smartphone.
  • CPR/First Aid preferred.
  • Spanish or bilingual skills are a plus.
