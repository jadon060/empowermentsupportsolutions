ESS Events!

ESS events are designed to celebrate participants, build community, create positive memories, and give each person something exciting to look forward to. Events may include birthday celebrations, holiday activities, annual outings, community updates, and public events connected to Regional Centers and DDS.

🎂 Birthdays 🎄 Holidays 🎢 Annual Outings 📰 Public News 🤝 Community Connection

What this page is for

This page gives participants and families a simple place to view upcoming ESS event ideas, annual traditions, holiday plans, participant celebrations, and public updates connected to DDS, Regional Centers, and disability-related community events. Details may change each year based on participant interest, safety planning, weather, transportation, staffing, and family coordination.

Featured ESS Events

Fun, safe, and participant-centered 🎉
🎂

Participant Birthday Celebration

Celebrated during the participant’s birthday month

ESS celebrates participant birthdays in a meaningful and personal way. The goal is to make each participant feel valued, included, and appreciated.

Great lunch: The participant is treated to a special lunch at an approved restaurant or preferred food location.
Birthday gifts: The participant may receive thoughtful gifts based on their interests, needs, and preferences.
Personalized celebration: Each birthday celebration may look different depending on the participant’s comfort level, schedule, and family input.
🎄

Christmas Event Vote

Participant vote planned before the holiday season

For Christmas, ESS will allow participants to vote on what type of holiday celebration they would prefer. This keeps the event participant-centered and gives everyone a voice.

Option 1: A Christmas party with food, activities, music, and time together as a group.
Option 2: A personalized gift basket made around each participant’s likes, needs, and interests.
Participant choice: ESS will collect participant votes and use the results to plan the holiday event.
🎢

Annual Participant Outing

Planned once per year, usually during late fall or early winter

The annual participant outing is a special ESS tradition where participants get the chance to enjoy a major community outing together, free of charge to participants.

Possible locations: Disneyland, Knott’s Berry Farm, Six Flags, museums, aquariums, or other major community attractions.
Changes yearly: The location may rotate each year so participants can experience different places over time.
No cost to participants: ESS plans these outings so participants can enjoy the experience without paying for admission.

Safety & Coordination Comes First

Before any ESS event or outing, participants and families will receive detailed instructions for safety, transportation, meeting times, food planning, supervision, emergency contacts, what to bring, what not to bring, and any other important coordination details. ESS will make sure participants and families understand the plan before the event takes place.

Participant-Centered Planning

ESS events are planned with participant choice in mind. Some participants may enjoy large group events, while others may prefer quieter, more personalized celebrations. ESS will consider comfort level, support needs, accessibility, transportation, family input, and each person’s preferences when planning events.

Family & Support Team Updates

Families and support teams may receive updates before events, including dates, locations, expectations, permission details when needed, and any safety notes. ESS will also provide reminders as events get closer so everyone has enough time to prepare.

Public News, DDS Updates & Community Events

This section is for public updates connected to DDS, Regional Centers, disability services, and community events. ESS may use this area to share general public news, event reminders, awareness days, and opportunities that may be helpful or meaningful for participants and families.

DDS Public Updates General public updates from the Department of Developmental Services, including announcements, community information, program updates, and public meetings.

Regional Center Events Public events or announcements shared by Regional Centers, including workshops, fairs, trainings, family events, or community activities.
🌎
Community Awareness Events Public events connected to inclusion, disability awareness, Earth Day, health, safety, recreation, and community participation.

Examples of Public Event Topics

ESS may share information about public DDS-related events, Regional Center events, Earth Day activities, disability awareness events, community resource fairs, safety workshops, transportation trainings, and recreation opportunities that are open to participants or families.

Event Details May Change

Dates, locations, and event details may change depending on participant needs, scheduling, transportation, staffing, safety, weather, ticket availability, and family coordination. Final event details will always be shared before the event.
