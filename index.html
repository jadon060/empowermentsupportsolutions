About Us

Empowerment Support Solutions LLC (ESS) is dedicated to helping individuals in California’s Self-Determination Program (SDP) thrive. ESS is guided by Person-Centered Thinking (PCT) principles, meaning we focus on the unique strengths, preferences, and goals of every individual we support. Our services are not one-size-fits-all—we tailor our approach to each person’s specific needs, ensuring that supports are meaningful, respectful, and empowering. By collaborating with families and honoring individual voices, we provide staffing and community-based services that promote independence, stability, and opportunities for people to live their best lives, their way.

Our Services

Independent Living Support

Daily one-on-one support designed around each participant’s strengths, preferences, and goals— helping people build confidence, skills, and independence at their own pace.

Staff Placement

We provide dependable staff you can count on—trained to follow PCT practices, honor individual routines, and collaborate closely with families for consistency and trust.

SDP Coordination

We partner with Independent Facilitators and FMS providers to ensure services are not only delivered smoothly but also connected to the person’s vision of a meaningful life.
