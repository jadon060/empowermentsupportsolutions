About Us

Empowerment Support Solutions LLC is dedicated to helping individuals in California’s Self-Determination Program (SDP) thrive. We provide reliable staffing and community-based support that helps families achieve independence and stability.

Our Services

Independent Living Support

Daily one-on-one support tailored to the participant’s goals, from community access to skill-building.

Staff Placement

We provide trained staff you can count on—consistent schedules, goal-oriented services, and family collaboration.

SDP Coordination

We work alongside Independent Facilitators and FMS providers to ensure smooth operations and billing.
