How Eric Earns Points

Points are earned by active participation during sessions and positive follow-through with goals. This keeps things motivating, simple, and easy to track.

+1 Point — Showing Up Ready Arrives on time, is prepared, and starts session with a good attitude.
+2 Points — Active Participation Joins in, answers questions, practices skills, and stays engaged.
+2 Points — Respectful Communication Uses calm words, listens, and communicates appropriately during session.
+3 Points — Completing a Task Finishes a planned activity like budgeting, community practice, scheduling, or daily living tasks.
+4 Points — Extra Effort Tries something new, stays focused, or pushes through frustration in a positive way.
