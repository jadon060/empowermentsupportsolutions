How Omar Earns Points

This system is designed to reward healthy habits, routine follow-through, participation in services, and steady ILS growth. Points are earned through real effort, not handed out automatically.

Workout & Healthy Living
+1 Point — Completes a Workout Finishes a planned workout, exercise session, walk, or other approved physical activity.
+1 Point — Makes a Healthy Food Choice Chooses a healthier meal, snack, or drink instead of a less healthy option.
+2 Points — Stays Consistent With Health Routine Follows through with multiple healthy choices in the same day such as exercise, hydration, or meal balance.
+2 Points — Shows Effort Toward Fitness Goal Puts in real effort toward a personal health goal like endurance, strength, routine building, or weight management.
Therapy & Psychiatric Follow-Through
+2 Points — Attends Weekly Therapy Session Shows up and participates in his scheduled weekly therapy appointment.
+2 Points — Attends Weekly Psych Session Shows up and participates in his scheduled weekly psychiatric or medication management appointment.
+1 Point — Talks About Mental Health Goals Engages in a healthy conversation about emotional wellness, coping, or treatment goals.
ILS Progress
+1 Point — Showing Up Ready Arrives on time, participates, and starts session with a cooperative attitude.
+2 Points — Active Participation in ILS Stays engaged in teaching, answers questions, and puts in visible effort.
+2 Points — Completing an ILS Task Finishes an assigned activity such as scheduling, communication practice, healthy planning, or community-based skill work.
+3 Points — Strong Follow-Through Applies the skill outside of session, remembers expectations, or demonstrates improved independence.

Important Point Policy

ESS staff and Omar’s family have full authority to add, deny, adjust, or remove points based on Omar’s effort, honesty, participation, follow-through, healthy choices, and overall progress. Points are earned through real effort and consistency and may be adjusted if expectations are not followed.
